Brief Bio

I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Austin. I completed my Master of Science in Computer Science from New York University's Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences in 2016, and my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi in 2014.

I am broadly interested in machine learning, and its applications to fields like natural language processing and robotics.

In my free time, I like sketching, and writing on Quora .

Previously, I have interned in Google's speech team, and in NVIDIA's autonomous driving team.
