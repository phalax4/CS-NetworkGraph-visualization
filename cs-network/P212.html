Note that M is at least 0 and less than n. Note also that "^" denotes the "to the power of". ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ Student Name: Student Section: ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ Solution: M^7 mod n = (M^6 mod n) * M mod n = ((M^3 mod n)^2 mod n) * M mod n = (((M^2 mod n) * M mod n)^2 mod n) * M mod n
